About our Scooba Life

The top provider of diving and watersports in Abu Dhabi is Scooba Life. We were established in Abu Dhabi in 2001 and were first founded in that year. Today, everyone is familiar with our name. We have more than 80 committed employees and are the largest operator in the area. Hani Nasir has served as managing director since 2001, while Abazar Nasir has served as general manager since 2008. Scooba Life has four commercial divisions: Courses & Charters for Diving Watersports Reselling and distributing Aquatic Workshop

How we act Including the prestigious diving operation in the Abu Dhabi Aquarium and Underwater Zoo, we proudly run four dive centers. On Yas Island, there are two watersports centers and a cutting-edge scuba workshop. Currently, we oversee ten or more wholesale customers in Dubai and Abu Dhabi. Scooba Life strives for excellence in all that it does by: Collaborating with the top global brands delivering improved services delivering unrivaled professionalism, dependability, and safety.
