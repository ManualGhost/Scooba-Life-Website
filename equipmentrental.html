Equipment Rental

Rental gear Scooba Life Diving has a little different policy on rental gear than most dive centers. We rent the same top of the line gear that we have available for sale in our dive center. All of our rental gear comes from manufacturers like Seac, Scubapro and Zeagle. Our standard rental BCDs are the Seac and Scubapro. These BCDs offer a back inflation system, weight integration, and an Air2 octo-inflator for comfort and convenience. Rental regulators include Scubapro and Zeagle. ScubaPro masks, fins, tanks and weight are available too, This is the same top quality, high performance gear that you’ll find in the showroom. For comfort and a wide range of fit we have a selection of 3mm to 5mm Bare wetsuits as well as 5mm Scubapro Everflex suits in our rental inventory.
